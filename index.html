Baked Feta Pasta

Baked feta pasta is a delightful and simple dish that is sure to be a crowd pleaser.
It's creamy, tangy, and can be made with just a few dishes for an easy cleanup.

Ingredients

  • 1 Block of Feta Cheese
  • 1 Package of cherry tomatoes
  • 1 Package of your choice of pasta, cooked
  • Cooking Oil (your choice)
  • Spices (salt, pepper, oinon, gerlic)
  • Chicken (optional)
  • Spinach

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. In a large baking dish, place block of feta in the center and arrange the tomatoes around it.
  3. Drizzle cooking oil over the cheese and tomatoes and season to taste.
  4. Place baking dish in oven and let cook until cheese is slightly melted and tomatoes are blistered (about 25 minutes
  5. Remove baking dish from oven and mix in cooked pasta. If desired, add chicken and spinach.
